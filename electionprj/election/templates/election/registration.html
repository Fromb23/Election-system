What are the Requirements to Register Online?

  • Kenyan Citizenship: Must be a citizen of Kenya.
  • Age Requirement: Must be at least 18 years old.
  • Registered Voter: Must be registered as a voter with the Independent Electoral and Boundaries Commission (IEBC).
  • Valid National ID or Passport: Must have a valid national ID or Kenyan passport.
  • Residence or Registered Polling Station: Must be registered to vote at a specific polling station.
  • Sound Mind: Must be of sound mind and not declared legally insane.
  • No Disqualifying Conviction: Should not be disqualified from voting due to an electoral offense or other legal disqualification.
